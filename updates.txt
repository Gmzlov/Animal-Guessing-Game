# Updates

## BETA Version 0.1 - [21/8/2023]

- Initial release of the Animal Guessing Game.
- Allows players to guess animals based on their starting letters.
- Three categories of animals: water, wild, and sky.
- Players have three attempts to guess the correct animal.
- Incorrect guesses reveal additional letters of the animal's name.
- Case-insensitive input for guessing.
- Displays the correct animal after three incorrect attempts.
